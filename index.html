Meeting of the Minds

A book club at Adventures of the Mind

Connect with fellow readers!

We are the meeting of the minds, a book club located at and run by Adventures of the Mind bookstore. We meet the first Saturday of every month and will have the book of the month in stock for you until that time! All we ask is that you please consider purchasing the book of the month from us (unless you own it already). We announce next month's book a week before the monthly meeting (this makes it easier for you to pick up a copy when we meet).
